All About Us

Fashion is the armor to survive the reality of everyday life - Bill Cunningham

  • Wonderland was founded in 2008 and is based in Pune. We create unique designs to meet each and every one of our customer's requirements.
  • With every passing year our business is expanding thanks to our loyal customers. We intend to keep the standards high and make no compromise to the quality of our products.
  • In the coming future we hope to bring a new fashion era, where we all can bring out our exclusive personalities through our unique and fashionable styles.
  • Every season, Wonderland introduces collections that embrace the hottest trends from across the globe. Whether stepping into a Wonderland store or shopping online, customers can look forward to a seamless and hassle-free shopping experience.
